Brick Art Instructor

Brick Art Instructor is an app for iOS and macOS to convert your pictures in easy to follow build instructions for your favourite construction bricks. The app is the result of my personal interest and fascination of Lego mosaic maker. I want to share an easier access to custom brick art you can build with the parts you already have and hope to find it useful.

Privacy Policy

Brick Art Instructor for iOS and macOS does not collect any data and cannot share any data with other parties.

Remarks

When exporting a project a fixed resolution copy of your source image is saved in unencrypted form. It can be easily restored, so please keep in mind that you save your image and share it as part of your project.
